This episode has another meme sourced from a comic. You've seen the "All the Things" meme, you probably still don't know it comes from This is Why I'll Never be an Adult. Good post, @AllieBrosh.

Know Your Meme describes it with this hilariously dry sentence: "The comic describes the author's frustration with her inability to maintain a consistent enthusiasm for her daily responsibilities."

The educational part of this comic (aw shit, I educated again) is mostly accurate. In actuality, it is not nuclear *waste* that is used to irradiate crops. Cobalt-60 is the most common way to achieve radiation breeding and it's produced for that purpose, it's not waste. But it is sometimes created by exposing cobalt-59 to nuclear power reactors.

This is all stuff I learned on Wikipedia. The best part of the Wikipedia article on cobalt-60 is this:

"The truck was recovered shortly after, but it was discovered that the thieves had removed the [cobalt-60] source from its shielding. The source was found abandoned and intact in a field close by, and authorities stated that the thieves were 'likely doomed'."
