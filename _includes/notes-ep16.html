The Abbey Road album cover is already viral enough to be a meme on its own. In this episode I got to revisit what a meme is.

To count, a meme really should mutate and have several competing variations. That hasn't happened much in previous episodes because it's not really necessary for the story, but this time it helps highlight the upshot: Monsanto. Since they modify genes it made sense to have the characters driving past modified memes.

All the backgrounds came from just one page on knowyourmeme.com. I picked my favorites. Check them out, link your favorites in the comments.
